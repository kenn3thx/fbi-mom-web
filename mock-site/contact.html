Before You Contact Us

For Faster Support

  • Check our Knowledge Base for instant answers
  • Have your account email ready
  • Note your child's device type and OS version
  • Try restarting the app before contacting support

Emergency Situations

If you believe your child is in immediate danger online, please:

  1. Contact local law enforcement if there's an immediate threat
  2. Report to the CyberTipline for online exploitation
  3. Reach out to our 24/7 emergency line: 1-800-555-EMERGENCY

FBI Mom provides tools for prevention and monitoring, but cannot intervene in active dangerous situations.
